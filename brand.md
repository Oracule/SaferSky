Professional dark-mode one-page website with a restrained, minimalist aesthetic and a premium visual tone.

Primary background: deep charcoal #0F1115
Secondary background sections: soft black #151922
Elevated surfaces/cards: muted graphite #1B2130

Primary text: high-contrast off-white #E6EAF2
Secondary text: cool gray #A8B0C0
Muted captions: slate gray #6B7280

Accent color (used sparingly for CTA, links, and interactive states): refined electric blue #3B82F6
Accent hover state: #2563EB
Subtle borders/dividers: low-contrast line #232A3A

Design principles:

Strict dark theme with controlled contrast

Flat surfaces, no heavy shadows

Subtle 1px borders instead of glow effects

Clean geometric layout with 8px spacing system

Generous whitespace and strong visual hierarchy

Modern sans-serif typography with clear scale

Smooth, understated transitions (150–250ms ease-in-out)

Minimal animations, focused on micro-interactions

No gradients unless extremely subtle

No visual clutter

Overall impression: elegant, restrained, high-end, and confidently minimal.
